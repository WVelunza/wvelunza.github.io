Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of Wilfred Velunza Web Design & Development. This policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you use our website or services.

Data Storage and Security

  • Contact form submissions are stored temporarily in our website's database

  • We use secure third-party services for specific functions:

Payment Security & Processing

We take the security of your payment information seriously. Here's how we protect your payment data:

  • We do not collect, store, or process credit card information directly on our website
  • All payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment provider
  • Payment transactions are conducted on Stripe's secure platform using industry-standard encryption
  • We maintain PCI compliance by never handling credit card data directly
  • All payment pages are served over HTTPS with SSL encryption

When making payments, you will be directed to Stripe's secure payment platform. Any payment information you provide is governed by Stripe's privacy policy and security practices. We encourage you to review Stripe's privacy policy before proceeding with any payment.

Client Project Information

  • We do not maintain ongoing databases of client websites or projects
  • We maintain confidentiality of client information and do not share content outside our organization
  • Project-specific credentials and sensitive information are handled with appropriate security measures
