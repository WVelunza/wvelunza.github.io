Service Delivery & Fulfillment Policies

Last updated: May 11, 2025

1. Initial Consultation

  • Free consultation to discuss your needs and project scope
  • Project timeline and milestones established
  • Written proposal provided with clear deliverables

2. Development Process

  • Regular progress updates via email
  • Opportunity to review and provide feedback at each stage
  • Unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied with the result

3. Final Delivery

  • Complete testing of all website functionality
  • Transfer of all necessary files and credentials
  • Training on how to use your new website (if requested)

Revision Policy

  • Unlimited revisions during the development phase until client approval
  • Post-launch modifications available at our standard hourly rate
  • Bug fixes related to our delivered work are provided at no additional cost

Project Cancellation

  • You may cancel the project at any time with written notice
  • We will complete the current milestone before ending the project
  • Payment is due for all work completed up to the cancellation date
  • Written notification required for project cancellation

Refund Policy

As our services are custom work:

  • Full refunds are available if we haven't begun work on your project
  • Partial refunds may be provided for incomplete milestones
  • Completed and delivered work is not eligible for refunds
  • All refund requests must be submitted in writing

Project Timeline

  • Each project timeline is custom based on scope and complexity
  • Specific delivery dates will be outlined in your project proposal
  • Regular updates provided throughout the development process
  • Timeline adjustments may be needed for scope changes or extensive revisions

Payment Terms

  • Services billed either hourly or per project as agreed in writing
  • All payments processed securely through Stripe
  • Additional charges apply for scope changes or extra features
  • Emergency support available at hourly rate based on availability
